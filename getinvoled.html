Our Outreach Programs provide free assistance to those struggling in the surrounding communities in the form of food, clothing, furniture, childrens’ programs and more.

Adopt-A-Block

Adopt-A-Block’s mission is to adopt neighborhoods that have been ravished by drugs, crime, and despair. We go into neighborhoods weekly for neighborhood clean ups, home repairs and maintenance for elderly and low income families, park beautification, block parties, food and clothes distribution and much more!

Food Distribution

The food distribution ministry aims to help families and children to have basic food needs met. This in turn opens doors to build meaningful relationships and help us to connect families to other services they might need such as education, and job training. Our Food Distributions consist of: Children’s Feeding Program: Monday-Friday our team prepares 200-300 meals a day ,sometimes more. To distribute to children in dire need of healthy meals.

S.H.I.N.E

Spreading Hope In Neighborhoods Everywhere is what its all about! SHINE is a fun sidewalk Sunday school ministry that reaches out to children in our local neighborhoods. Through this program we introduce children to the concept of church and community through games and skits. Come out and SHINE with us!
